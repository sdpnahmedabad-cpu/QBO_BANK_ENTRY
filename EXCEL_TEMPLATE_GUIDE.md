# Excel/CSV Bank Statement Template

Use this template to upload your bank transactions without needing PDF parsing.

## Instructions:

1. **Download this template** or create a new Excel file
2. **Fill in your transaction data** following the column format below
3. **Save as .xlsx or .csv**
4. **Upload to the Bank Entries page**

## Column Format:

| Date       | Description           | Amount  | Transaction Type |
|------------|-----------------------|---------|------------------|
| 01/01/2026 | Amazon.com Purchase   | 50.00   | Expense          |
| 02/01/2026 | Salary Deposit        | 3000.00 | Income           |
| 03/01/2026 | Grocery Store         | 125.50  | Expense          |

## Column Definitions:

- **Date**: DD/MM/YYYY format
- **Description**: Transaction description
- **Amount**: Positive number (no currency symbols)
- **Transaction Type**: Either "Income" or "Expense"

## Tips:

- ✅ Use Excel or Google Sheets
- ✅ Keep amounts as positive numbers
- ✅ Use DD/MM/YYYY date format
- ✅ Mark Income vs Expense in the type column
- ❌ Don't include currency symbols ($, €, etc.)
- ❌ Don't include commas in numbers

## Alternative: Export from Your Bank

Most banks allow you to export transactions as Excel or CSV directly. Look for:
- "Export to Excel"
- "Download as CSV"
- "Export Transactions"

This is the **fastest and most reliable** way to import your data!
